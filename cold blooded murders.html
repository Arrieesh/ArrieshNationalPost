'Cold blooded murderers': Two dead, more than 20 wounded, in shooting outside Florida banquet hall

The United States saw at least 200 mass shootings in the first 132 days of this year

Assailants opened fire on a crowd outside a concert in Florida early on Sunday, killing two people and wounding more than 20, police and media reports said.

Three people got out of a white SUV and fired assault-style weapons and handguns at revelers gathered at the El Mula Banquet Hall near Hialeah, a city in Miami-Dade county, according to local media.
